1. Eevee

      #133

      • Normal

      Eevee is the game mascot and starter Pokémon in Pokémon: Let's Go, Eevee!, as well as for the main characters of Pokémon XD: Gale of Darkness and Pokémon Conquest. It is the starting Pokémon and first Pokémon employee of the player in Pokémon Café Mix. It is also the rival's starter Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow, although Professor Oak originally intended to give it to the player.

    2. Vaporeon

      #134

      • Water

      Vaporeon, the first of the Eeveelutions in National Pokédex order, is a Water-type Pokémon. It evolves from an Eevee that is exposed to a Water Stone. Vaporeon is the only Eeveelution that does not share any weakness with another.

    3. Jolteon

      #135

      • Electric

      Jolteon is the second of the Eeveelutions, evolving from exposure to a Thunder Stone, and is an Electric-type Pokémon. Jolteon is the only Eeveelution without an obvious tail. However, in the The Electric Tale of Pikachu manga, it is shown to have a stubby tail under its spikes, although the tail is absent from its Generation V backsprite.

    4. Flareon

      #136

      • Fire

      The final member of Generation I's Eeveelution trio is Flareon, a Fire-type Pokémon, evolving from Eevee when it is exposed to a Fire Stone. Flareon is the only Eeveelution with a type advantage over more than one other Eeveelution.

    5. Espeon

      #196

      • Psychic

      The Psychic-type Espeon evolves from Eevee when it is raised to a friendship of 220 or higher and gains a level during the daytime or if the player has a Sun Shard (Pokémon XD: Gale of Darkness). It, alongside Umbreon, is the starter Pokémon for Pokémon Colosseum.

    6. Umbreon

      #197

      • Dark

      Umbreon is the second of the two Generation II Eeveelutions, a Dark-type Pokémon. Umbreon evolves from Eevee when it has a friendship of 220 or higher and gains a level during the nighttime or if the player has a Moon Shard (Pokémon XD: Gale of Darkness). It, alongside Espeon, is the starter Pokémon for Pokémon Colosseum.

    7. Leafeon

      #470

      • Grass

      Leafeon is the Grass-type evolution of Eevee. When an Eevee is leveled up near a large, mossy rock (from Generations IV through VII) or exposed to a Leaf Stone (in Generation VIII), it will evolve into this Pokémon.

    8. Glaceon

      #471

      • Ice

      Glaceon is an Ice-type evolution of Eevee. Glaceon evolves from Eevee when it is leveled up near an ice-covered rock (from Generations IV through VII and in Pokémon Brilliant Diamond and Shining Pearl) or exposed to an Ice Stone (in Pokémon Sword and Shield and Pokémon Legends: Arceus).

    9. Sylveon

      #700

      • Fairy

      Sylveon is a Fairy-type evolution of Eevee. It evolves when leveled up with a Fairy-type move and at least two Affection hearts in Pokémon-Amie or Pokémon Refresh, or in Generation VIII, when leveled up with a Fairy-type move and at least 160 base happiness.
